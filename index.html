About us
Lenek Game Development is a small game development studio from Poland, specialized in creating tycoon/simulator games for Roblox.

We develop free Roblox games, played and enjoyed by millions of users from all around the World. Our main goal is to provide unique, high-quality experiences, adapted to the expectations of players.
History
Lenek Game Development was founded in 2017, by a single, self-taught Roblox game developer, with a huge passion for creating games.

Since then, we have released 3 high-quality simulation games: Bitcoin Tycoon (2018), remastered w 2022, Esports Empire (2019) and Developer Inc (2022). We constantly work on expanding and improving our projects.
